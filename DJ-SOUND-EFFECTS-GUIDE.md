## 📂 Where to Find FREE DJ Sound Effects

### 🆓 Free Resources
1. **Freesound.org** - Massive database of free samples
   - Search: "air horn", "scratch", "rewind", "siren"
   - Creative Commons licensed

2. **Zapsplat.com** - Free with account
   - Professional DJ sound effects library
   - Multiple format downloads

3. **Adobe Audition** - Comes with DJ effects pack
   - If you have Creative Cloud subscription

4. **FL Studio Demo** - Free demo includes samples
   - Lots of hip-hop and electronic effects

5. **BandLab** - Free online DAW with sample library
   - Good for finding transition effects

### 🎵 Genre-Specific Sources

**Hip-Hop/Rap:**
- Search for: "DJ Premier scratch", "hip hop air horn", "DJ drop"
- Classic samples from 90s/2000s mixtapes

**Electronic/Rave:**
- Search for: "festival siren", "laser sweep", "rave horn"
- Look for festival/big room house samples

**Reggaeton/Latin:**
- Search for: "reggaeton horn", "latin party", "dale sample"
- Puerto Rican DJ drops and party calls

**Trap:**
- Search for: "trap buildup", "808 drop", "trap transition"
- Producer tag samples (avoid copyrighted ones)

## 🛠️ How to Integrate with MEW

### 🎵 File Format Requirements
- **Format:** MP3 or WAV
- **Length:** 1-10 seconds ideal
- **Quality:** 44.1kHz/16-bit minimum
- **Volume:** Normalized/consistent levels

### 📁 Suggested File Organization

## 💡 Pro Tips

### 🎵 For Best Results
1. **Match Effect to Genre** - Hip-hop gets scratches, electronic gets lasers
2. **Time with Energy Changes** - Big energy jumps = big effects
3. **Vary Your Techniques** - MEW already does this automatically!
4. **Read the Room** - Party crowds love air horns, chill crowds prefer smooth

### 🔊 Sound Design Tips
- **Layer Effects** - Multiple sounds create fuller transitions
- **EQ Your Effects** - High-pass filters prevent mud
- **Compress Consistently** - Even levels across all effects
- **Test on Speakers** - What sounds good in headphones might not translate
